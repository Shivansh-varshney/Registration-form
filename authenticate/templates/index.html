The admission process will be in 3 phases:

Phase 1: Registrations.

In phase 1, students have to register themselves on this CSAS portal. Details required are as below

  1. Family Details
  2. Personal Details
  3. Secondary Education Details
  4. Bank details for refund in case candidate fails to secure admission.

Phase 2: Preference list

In phase 2, students will give their preference list of course and college combinations for the admission.

From July 1,2024 12:00 AM to July 5, 2024 11:59 PM

Phase 3: Declaration of Seats.

On July 8, 2024 at 5pm the alloted seats will be declared. Students who would have been alotted a seat shall accept the seat and pay college fee to secure admission. If a student fails to submit the fee on time his/her seat will be cancelled.
